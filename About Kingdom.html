Kingdom

Kingdom is a show about the past china where a danger of zombie apocalypse emerges and mainly about the charcters who plays different roles effecting of affecting the threat.

In past the main kingdom of china is facing a famine and the king is sick. This is brewring a tense situation among the people and relatives of the king. His wife is pregnant and his another son is in a position of becoming a king if he current king dies.

This leads to several betrayls and a horrific truth about the experiment they did with the flower of resurecction which after many situations lead to people becoming zombie and the apocalypse begun.

The story is filled with many twist and turn and many bone chilling plot twists. It is a must watch for any who enjoys thriller, horror, beautiful potrayal, beautifully paces and told story.
